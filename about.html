Origin Story

H-Thai-ML for the first time, you are transported to the exotic land of the Thais. The restaurant's beautiful interior reflects the harmonious balance of contemporary simplicity and classical elegance. Cut and patterned panels of deep mahogany and other wood frame walls of vibrant color. Lustrous Thai silks and exquisite classical Thai paintings sparkle and delight. Take a few moments to stroll through the delightful haven.
